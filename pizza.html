Good to know.

      

        
How much space do you need?
A flat spot of roughly 3×2 metres, outdoors or well covered, plus a sturdy table. The oven runs on gas — no power hookup needed, no smoke worries.

        
What if it rains?
A covered spot is all we need — carport, pavilion, barn entrance or a big balcony all work. For summer events I recommend planning a small shady/rainy plan B anyway.

        
How many pizzas should we plan?
Count on 1 to 1.5 pizzas per person — during the service window it's all-you-can-eat anyway, so nobody leaves hungry.

        
Vegetarian, vegan or gluten-free?
Vegetarian is always covered. Vegan mozzarella and gluten-free dough are no problem with a few days notice — just mention it in your request.

        
How far do you travel?
I am based in Bern. Up to 60 km travel is included in every package, further afield is possible by arrangement.

        
Deposit & cancellation?
Your date is fixed with a 20% deposit. Free cancellation up to 14 days before the event — after that we simply find a new date.
